About Me

I'm an avid lover of the outdoors, camping, hiking, and mountain biking. I love to spend my free time with my family. I began coding as a hobby and felt I needed to do more with my hobby. So the decision was made to use my GI Benefits to get a degree in Web Design and Development. I also plan on getting a seperate degree in Marketing Communications.

I spent 12 years in the military and during that time held various military occupations. I found great satisfaction in the military and often miss the comradery that can only be found in the military. I did serve one Tour of Duty in Iraq.

The Genesis

This page is a culmination of different code languages I have learned at college and what I have been able to teach myself. HTML5 covers the most basic layout. In fact this text you are reading is nested in my HTML source code. I am using CSS to lay down some basic styles to my HTML elements. I decided to use BootStrap for the first time with this page. The Exodus

I began my journey several years ago when I decided to leave the military. Had I known at the time that Americas economy was in shambles I perhaps might of stayed in. Anyway, I jumped around from job to job never really finding the kind of satisfaction I did when I was in the military. In my spare time I found myself creating small web pages and snippets of code. It gave me great satisfaction to be able to see my work. It seemed liked it was my calling so I decided to enroll in classes for Web Design and Development at Camden County College. Here I am today explaining my journey to you on my own webpage.
